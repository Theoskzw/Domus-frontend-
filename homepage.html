About DOMUS

DOMUS simplifies apartment living for residents by offering a comprehensive suite of tools and services. From managing maintenance requests to staying updated with community notices, we ensure a hassle-free living experience with modern technology and exceptional service. Our platform is designed to create a sense of community while addressing the practical needs of modern apartment living.

Key Features

Resident Management

Efficiently manage tenant information, lease agreements, and communication with an intuitive interface that keeps everything organized and accessible.

Maintenance Requests

Submit, track, and resolve maintenance issues with ease. Our streamlined system ensures quick response times and complete transparency throughout the resolution process.

Notifications

Receive instant updates and alerts about community events, important announcements, and the status of your maintenance requests through multiple channels.
